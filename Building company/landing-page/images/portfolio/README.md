# Portfolio Images

Organize your project photos here.

## Folder Structure

Each project has its own folder:
- `project-1/` — Bathroom renovation — Randwick
- `project-2/` — Kitchen and living — Maroubra
- `project-3/` — Timber deck — Coogee
- `project-4/` — Structural framing — Bondi
- `project-5/` — Office fit-out — Alexandria
- `project-6/` — Ensuite — Bronte

## What to Add

**Per project:** 3-6 high-resolution photos showing:
- Before shots (if available)
- During construction
- After/finished work
- Detail shots (joints, finishes, craftsmanship)

**File naming:** 
- `before-1.jpg`, `during-1.jpg`, `after-1.jpg`
- Or: `01.jpg`, `02.jpg`, `03.jpg` (in order)

**Recommended size:** 1200px wide minimum, 2000px+ ideal

**Tips:**
- Quality over quantity — only your best work
- Show variety: different angles, close-ups, wide shots
- Good lighting is essential
- These photos sell your credibility to qualified tradies
