# Open Graph / Social Sharing Images

Drop your social sharing image here.

**File name:** `og-image.jpg` (or `.png`)

**Recommended size:** 1200x630px (Facebook/LinkedIn standard)

**What to use:**
- Your logo + tagline
- Hero image with text overlay
- Company branding
- Professional job site photo

**Tips:**
- This appears when someone shares your site on social media
- Make sure text is readable at small sizes
- Include your company name/logo
- Update the `og:image` URL in `index.html` after adding
